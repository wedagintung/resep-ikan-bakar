Sambal is an Indonesian chili sauce or paste typically made from a mixture of a variety of chili peppers with secondary ingredients such as shrimp paste, garlic, ginger, shallot, scallion, palm sugar, and lime juice. Sambal is an Indonesian loan-word of Javanese origin (sambel). It is native to the cuisines of Indonesia, and popular in Malaysia, Sri Lanka, Brunei and Singapore.

Ikan Bakar Sambal Matah instruksinya

  1. Cuci bersih ikan kue lalu masukan bumbu marinasi diamkan 10 menit.
  2. Campurkan bumbu halus dengan lada, garam, gula, minyak wijen, saos tiram, dan minyak goreng aduk rata.
  3. Setelah bumbu tercampur oleskan rata pada ikan kue dan diamkan 15 menit.
  4. Panaskan tungku dan arang lalu bakar ikan kue sampai matang merata dan bumbu meresap.
  5. Tiriskan dan sajikan dengan sambal matah.
