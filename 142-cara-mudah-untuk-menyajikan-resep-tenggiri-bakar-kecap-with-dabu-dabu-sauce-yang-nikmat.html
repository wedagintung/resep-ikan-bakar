Dabu-dabu is a type of hot and spicy condiment commonly found in Manado cuisine of North Sulawesi, Indonesia. Dabu-dabu chopped red chili peppers, bird's eye chili, shallots, red and green tomatoes, and a pinch of salt and sugar. All of the chopped and diced spices are mixed with fresh calamansi juice or locally known as lemon cui or jeruk kesturi; sometimes calamansi is replaced by kaffir lime. Ikan bakar is an Indonesian and Malaysian dish, prepared with charcoal-grilled fish or other forms of seafood. Ikan bakar literally means "roasted fish" in Indonesian and Malay. The basic difference from other grilled fish, ikan bakar is usually served with sweet soy sauce. The barbecued fish is one of the classic Indonesian dish.

Tenggiri Bakar Kecap with Dabu-dabu Sauce langkah -langkahnya

  1. Potong dan bersihkan ikan tenggiri. Peraskan jeruk nipis dan oleskan merata..
  2. Haluskan bawang putih dan ketumbar. Sebagian oleskan pada ikan. Dan sebagian tambahkan kecap manis..
  3. Panggang ikan menggunakan teflon hingga setengah matang dan oleskan bumbu kecap di kedua sisi nya hingga matang..
  4. Lalu mulai membuat sambal dabu-dabu. iris semua bahan dabu-dabu dan campurkan dengan memberi sedikit olive oil hingga merata..
  5. Sajikan ikan tengiri bakar kecap dan tuangkan sambal dabu-dabu di atas nya. Siap untuk di santap..
